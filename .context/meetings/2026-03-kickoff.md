- Normal 2-week sprints
- Every sprint ships at least one testable change or prototype tied to UXT (either squad)
- Each hypothesis = workstream; Acquisition vs. Activation split is by surface, but goals/metrics/learning are shared
- Bias: fast + opinionated changes + qual + holdout. Reserve full A/Bs for the spiciest bets.

## Measurement Recap Shared at Kickoff

Traditional A/B approach (50/50 split, wait for stat sig) is too slow for UXT pace.

New approach:
- 10% holdout group → never sees any new UXT releases
- 90% treatment group → receives all UXT releases over time via staged rollouts

This gives a clean cumulative read on program impact without blocking delivery.
