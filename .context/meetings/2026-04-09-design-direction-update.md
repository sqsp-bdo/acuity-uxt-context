# Design Direction Update: Hybrid Concept

**Date**: April 9, 2026  
**From**: Luenne Neri (Designer, Acquisition squad)  
**Status**: In progress — directional signal from UXR testing, not a final decision

## Direction

Based on UXR testing run on both concepts, the likely direction is a **hybrid** of Concept A and Concept B rather than either one alone.

- **Concept A (template-first)** — template cards serve as a fast-path starting point for users who want speed
- **Concept B (step-by-step AI-guided)** — stepped flow powers the "create a new appointment type" path for users who want to build their own

The two concepts are expected to coexist in the final design.

## Open Question Under Exploration

Luenne is exploring pushing Concept A further to allow **selecting and adding multiple appointment templates** upfront — not just one.

## Implications

- The stepped flow (Concept B) is not the only path. Template selection (Concept A) is a valid fast-path entry that should not be removed.
- Engineering should expect to support both paths: a template-based selection flow and a guided creation flow.
- Multiple template selection is a potential scope addition — not confirmed, still being evaluated.
